Being Jhon Malkovich is Borderline Arthouse-SciFi-Fantasy movie which brings out really great questions about identity and soul at the same time mocking the main character for doing this. Protagonist is a guy who try to impress a beautiful co-worker but ultimately he turns out to be pretenious horny man. It mocks all those movies which are so called Arthouse and DEEEEP , so arthousy that one can't simply enjoys it. This movie itself belongs to arthouse genre but somehow it is a very very enjoyable movie. Thanks to charlie kaufman for writing such a creative movie in 1999. The concept is great although the mechanics of portal weren't explained properly and captain mertin backstory must be given more screentime.
